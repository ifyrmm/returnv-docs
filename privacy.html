Privacy Policy

Only information necessary for operating the bot is stored. Data may be removed upon request where applicable.

Third-Party Services

Our services rely on Discord APIs and may use trusted hosting providers to operate securely.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored information.

Contact
